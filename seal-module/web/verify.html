How Verification Works

Every sealed document carries a forensic fingerprint that makes tampering impossible to hide.

🔢 SHA-512 Match

We recompute the SHA-512 hash of the uploaded PDF. If it matches the embedded hash, the document is exactly as sealed -- no tampering possible.

⛓ Blockchain Check

The OTS digest embedded in the QR is sent to OpenTimestamps calendar servers. If Bitcoin has confirmed the timestamp, you get a green tick. Pending means the seal is valid but not yet blockchain-confirmed.

👤 Identity Card

If sender identity was included during sealing, we display it here for affidavit pre-fill and chain of custody tracking.

🔐 .voice Decryption

Password-protected documents produce a .voice encrypted file. Upload it here with the sender's password to decrypt and verify the original sealed PDF.

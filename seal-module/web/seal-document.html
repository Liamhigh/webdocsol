How Document Sealing Works

Every document sealed by Verum Omnis receives a forensic-grade cryptographic seal that makes it tamper-evident and court-admissible.

📜 A4 Watermark Background

The full-page Verum Omnis watermark is applied at 20% opacity behind your content. Your original document text is preserved exactly -- the QR code sits in the natural page margin.

📱 Clean QR Code

QR sits in the top-right margin with white background panel -- never covers document text. Original document layout is fully preserved. Scans instantly with any phone.

🔒 Dual Hash

SHA-256 for OpenTimestamps blockchain anchoring. SHA-512 as the Verum forensic fingerprint. Two independent hashes, zero trust required.

⛓ Bitcoin Blockchain

OpenTimestamps anchors the SHA-256 hash into Bitcoin. Once confirmed (~1-2 hours), the timestamp is permanently and independently verifiable.

👤 Identity Pipeline

Optional sender identity (name, ID, address, email) is written into your private Seal Certificate for affidavit pre-fill and chain of custody — never into the public QR code unless you explicitly opt in.

📍 GPS + Device

Automatic geolocation and device fingerprint capture. Proves where and from what device the seal was applied.

🔐 Password Protection

Optional AES-256 password protection with delivery receipt cover page. Recipient must email sender for password -- that email IS the read receipt.

🛡 Tamper Detection

Recipient uploads document to verify page. If SHA-512 doesn't match --> "TAMPERED -- DO NOT ACCEPT". Cryptographically impossible to forge.
